Our Services!

At Your Service

Transformers & Breakers

  • Rewinding (upto 33 KV, 5000 KVA).
  • Oil filtration.
  • Health check :
    • Turn ratio, Magnetic balance, PI Value, DAR Value, Tan Delta, PPM & Resistance Value Tests.
  • Overhauling for On Load Tap Changer (OLTC).
  • Protection Circuit :
    • Buckle stripping, Over-current & earth fault alarm & tripping check.

Electrical Motor Repair

  • Servicing, Overhauling & Rewinding of HT/LT, AC/DC motors, generators, alternators & welding sets.
  • Rewinding of eddy current drive & servo motors.
  • Rewinding of electro-magnetic clutch coils.
  • Manufacturing of control transformers.

Reliability & Field Services

  • Dynamic balancing of rotating machines.
  • Preventive maintenance (AMC).
  • Field Services (On-site) :
    • Motor Testing.
    • Predictive Maintenance.
    • Transformer Oil Filtration & Servicing.
    • Transformer Health Check.
